ğŸ§  What Is RFM Analysis?

RFM analysis is a dataâ€‘driven technique used to evaluate customer value based on three behavioral metrics:

Recency (R): How recently a customer made a purchase

Frequency (F): How often they make purchases

Monetary (M): How much money they spend

By scoring and combining these metrics, customers can be grouped into meaningful segments (e.g., Champions, Loyal, At Risk) that help businesses tailor messaging, promotions, and retention strategies.
